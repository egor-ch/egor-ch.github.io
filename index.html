Egor Chulkov

I am currently a PhD student at Skoltech under the supervision of Oleg Vasilyev. My research focuses on developing efficient, real-time, and operation-rich implicit modeling techniques for engineering and design applications. I am particularly interested in numerical methods and algorithms related to F-rep / SDF, with applications in rendering, discretization, and meshing.
